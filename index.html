Convenience Casinos

What is a convenience casino?

“We do not believe that property can support another $500 million destination casino on it,’’ Taylor said of the Tiverton site. “We look at this location as a convenience casino location, one where people can get into it and get out of it easily.” John Taylor, Chairman Twin River Management Group

A "convenience casino" is a lot like a convenience store — easy to get in and easy to get out. About 80% of people at convenience casinos are locals. 8 out of 10 people going in our locals. Your neighbors. Your friends. Money is not coming into the town from far and wide. Money is coming out of the pockets of your neighbors. Money is coming out of the pockets of your friends.

Payouts 101

What is a payout?

A payout is basically money returned to players. You don't win money. Casinos don't award money. Some portion of the money played is returned to players as payouts.

What is a payout percentage?

A payout percentage is how much money is returned to players over some length of time. These are a bit hard to find unless you know exactly what you're looking for but here are the RI lottery payout percentages. Let's take a look at August 2016.

Newport Grand had a payout percentage of 90.83%.

Wait, really? So casinos basically just keep a little of what's given to them? That's how they "make money"? Yep.

So — all things considered — the proposed Tiverton casino will take money from your neighbors and friends, and give back about 90% of it.

Don't give up 10% of your money — keep the casino out of Tiverton.
