Convenience Casinos

What is a convenience casino?

“We do not believe that property can support another $500 million destination casino on it,’’ Taylor said of the Tiverton site. “We look at this location as a convenience casino location, one where people can get into it and get out of it easily.” John Taylor, Chairman Twin River Management Group

A "convenience casino" is a lot like a convenience store — easy to get in and easy to get out. About 80% of people at convenience casinos are locals. 8 out of 10 people going in our locals. Payouts 101

What is a payout?

A payout is basically money returned to players. You don't win money. Casinos don't award money. Some portion of the money played is returned to players as payouts.

What is a payout percentage?

A payout percentage is how much money is returned to players over some length of time. These are a bit hard to find unless you know exactly what you're looking for but here are the RI lottery payout percentages.

Look at August 2016. Newport Grand had a payout percentage of 90.83%.

Put simply, this is the same as giving all of your money to someone and getting a chance to win about 90% of it back.
